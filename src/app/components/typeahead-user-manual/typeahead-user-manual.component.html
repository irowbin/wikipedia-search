Search Guide

1 Start Typing

As you type, a dropdown will appear showing suggested Wikipedia articles.

Example:
Search: "quantum ph"
Suggestions:
  • #1 Quantum computing
    Information on quantum computing

2 Select or Continue Typing

  • Click a suggestion to select it
  • Or keep typing to refine results
  • Scroll to bottom to load more results (100 items per page)

3 View Article

Click the article title to open it in a new tab.
